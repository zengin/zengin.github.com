Intro
I am a Software Engineer at Microsoft. I live in Vancouver, BC, Canada. I received my B.Sc. degree from Computer Engineering Department at Bilkent University in 2011 and my Master's Degree in Computer Science from Max Planck Institute for Software Systems.

Throughout my academic and industry experience, I had a chance to write code in almost every major programming language (e.g. C, C++, C#, Java, Go, Python, Javascript, Ocaml). Nowadays I am learning F#. My previous research focused on programming languages (PL) theory and parallel computing. I applied formal proof techniques from PL theory to prove correctness of a fault tolerance system in a distributed parallel computing setting.

I have a special interest in natural languages. Besides my native language Turkish, I speak English, German and Arabic. I am not fluent in the latter two, but they are part of my day-to-day personal improvement activities.

I love learning. I always spare time to study new topics in the areas of, including but not limited to, computer science, logic, semantics, psychology (especially psycho-lingustics), linguistics and cognitive aspects of learning.
